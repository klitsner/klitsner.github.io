My name is Samson Klitsner, and this is a bathtub made of free stuff from craigslist. I am a recent graduate of the Design | Media Arts program at UCLA where I learned to ask questions and channel ideas through interactive environments, motion/video and sound design. I aim to engage viewers' imaginative and creative sensibilities with a playful multimedia approach that combines design thinking with interactive platforms.

This is my personal website that I use to showcase my own work and other interesting projects I have participated in. I am currently working as an Interaction Designer and Sound Designer at Oblong Industries, but I am always happy to meet new people so if you want to get in touch for any reason, shoot me an email at sklitsner@gmail.com
